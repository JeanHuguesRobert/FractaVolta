The Guide answers from the **public corpus only**. It cites sources, separates what is certain from what still needs checking, and keeps local paths and private material out of view.

## How to use this page

1. Ask a question below or pick a suggested prompt.
2. Read the answer and its cited sources.
3. To go deeper, open **Deepen with your own agent**, copy the prepared prompt, and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, Grok, or another assistant.
4. When your agent proposes a follow-up question, paste it back here with **Ask the Guide again** or type it directly.

This is an assisted ping-pong loop: the Guide stays grounded in the public corpus; your agent can elaborate, compare, and help you decide what to ask next. Neither side silently changes the corpus.

## Boundaries

- Read-only public view.
- No account required.
- Conversation memory stays in your browser for seven days and can be cleared at any time.
- The Guide suggests and explains; it does not deploy, publish, or decide on your behalf.
