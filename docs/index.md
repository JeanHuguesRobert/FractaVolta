# FractaVolta

> From photons to inference

FractaVolta explores how to transform abundant solar energy into reliable, usable services.

Instead of centralized infrastructure, it applies distributed system patterns to energy:
- modular storage
- local decision-making
- redundancy
- graceful degradation

---

## Why it matters

Solar energy is abundant.

What is scarce:
- reliability
- timing
- quality of service

FractaVolta addresses this gap.
